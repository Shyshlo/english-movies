⭐ A moment for inspiration ⭐

Surely you are familiar with such a higher educational institution as Harvard. This university in the United States is one of the most successful and famous in the world. A Harvard diploma is a kind of indicator of talent and success. Among the famous alumni there are many outstanding people whose names are known all over the world: Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates, George W. Bush, Theodore Roosevelt and others.
So why not get inspired to learn by their phrases?
